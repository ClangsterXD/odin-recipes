Grilled Chicken Sandwich

Homepage

A classic grilled chicken sandwich (from McDonald's...).

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast: 1 (thawed)
  • Buns: whole-wheat recommended
  • Produce: 1 slice of tomato and 1 leaf of lettuce
  • Condiment: 1 tablespoon of mayonnaise
  • Seasoning:
    • 1/2 teaspoon olive oil
    • Pinch of salt
    • Pinch of black pepper
  • Plastic wrap

Steps

  1. Start heating a small skillet over medium-high heat
  2. While that hets, prep the chicken.
    • Pat the chicken dry.
    • Place it in plastic wrap and lightly pound it into even thickness.
    • Rub with seasonings listed above.
  3. Cook the chicken in the skillet for 5-6 minutes per side.
    4. until internal temp reaches 165°F and is golden brown.
  4. Toast the buns face-down in the pan for 1 minute.
  5. Place the grilled chicken, produce, and condiment between the buns.
  6. Serve immediately.
    7. Yummy!
