Simple One-Bowl Brownie

Homepage

A declious-looking softly baked brownie topped with chocolate syrup.

Ingredients

  • Butter: 1 stick
  • Granulated white sugar: 1 cup
  • Pure vanilla extract: 1 teaspoon
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder: 1/2 cup
  • All-purpose flour: 1/2 cup
  • Salt: 1/4 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Grease 8x8-inch pan with 1/2 teaspoon of butter
  3. Whisk rest of butter and sugar in a bowl until smooth
  4. Mix eggs and vanilla until fully incorporated
  5. Stir in cocoa powder, flour, and salt until combined
  6. Bake for 22-25 minutes
    7. Note: you can test if it's done with a toothpick! Just insert into the center and when pulling out a few moist crumbs should leave with it indicating it is complete.
