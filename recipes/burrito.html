Black Bean Burrito

Homepage

These simple burritos are a great addition to any day.

Ingredients

Note: The Ingredients below are for 1 burrito. Multiply as needed for quantity desired.

  • Tortilla: large
  • Black beans: 1/3 cup canned
  • Rice: 1/4 cup cooked
  • Avocado: 1/4 to 1/2 of ripe avocado (sliced or smashed)
  • Salsa: 1-2 tablespoons (tomato-based)
  • Seasoning (pinch of each):
    • Salt
    • Cumin
    • Chili powder
    • Black pepper
  • 2 damp paper towels

Steps

  1. Mix the beans, rice, salsa, and seasoning in a small bowl. Microwave for 34-60seconds until hot.
  2. Soften the Tortilla in the microwave for 10 seconds between two damp paper towels.
